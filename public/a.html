ShieldMail is a secure temporary email service that allows you to create disposable email addresses. When you create a ShieldMail address, you can enable email forwarding to your real email address. Any emails sent to your ShieldMail address will be automatically forwarded to your real inbox. The sender will only see your ShieldMail address, keeping your real email private. You can toggle forwarding on or off for each ShieldMail address individually.
